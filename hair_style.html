Hair Care Information

Professional tips and advice for maintaining healthy hair

Hair Care Tips

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo for color-treated hair
  • Apply conditioner primarily to the ends of your hair
  • Limit heat styling to prevent damage
  • Use heat protectant before blow-drying or styling
  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks
